In detail

Every feature, explained

Setup

Open in 30 seconds, close in under a minute

Gas struts take the effort out of setup — one smooth motion and the tent is fully open. No poles, no clips, no fumbling in the dark. Folding is a comfortable one-person job. A rubber band keeps the fabric neatly tucked when closed.

Materials

Aerospace-grade aluminum honeycomb shell

The same construction used in aviation: maximum strength at minimum weight. Resists dents, UV degradation, and corrosion. Won't crack in freezing temperatures, won't warp in summer heat. Outperforms fibreglass and ABS alternatives on all long-term durability metrics.

Sleep quality

World-first dual-layer mattress — 120 × 200 cm

A 3.5 cm firm base layer for support combined with a 3.5 cm soft top layer for comfort. The anti-condensation mat and waterproof bottom layer keep you dry and comfortable regardless of temperature swings. Washable cover included.

New for 2025

Bug-repelling LED mode + external heater port

The updated LED strip adds white, yellow, and red modes — red light attracts significantly fewer insects. A new circular heater port lets you connect an external heat source for cold-weather camping without compromising the tent's sealed structure.

Organization

Ventilated shoe bags, overhead net, side pockets

External shoe bags keep boots outside the sleeping area, with mesh bottoms to prevent moisture buildup overnight. An overhead storage net handles bulky gear, side pockets organize smaller items, and the telescopic ladder stows inside when closed.

Expandable

6 T-slot rails for accessories

Three T-slots on each side accept standard accessories — awnings, recovery boards, shovels, and more. Universal hardware means you're not locked into any proprietary ecosystem.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Will the Alduo fit my vehicle?
The Alduo fits most vehicles with roof rails or crossbars rated for at least 75 kg dynamic load. The mounting base is 210 × 130 cm with universal sliding brackets — compatible with Thule, Rhino-Rack, Yakima, ARB, and similar systems. No drilling required for most setups. Send us your vehicle make and model if you're unsure before ordering.
How long does setup really take?
Under 30 seconds to fully open — the gas struts do the work. Folding takes 1–2 minutes and is a comfortable one-person job. A rubber band helps tuck the fabric inside cleanly. Watch the setup video on this page to see a real-world demo — not a sped-up marketing version.
Can I leave the tent on my car year-round?
Yes. The aluminum honeycomb shell is corrosion-resistant and UV-stable, designed for permanent mounting. The closed height adds approximately 20 cm to your vehicle height — keep this in mind for underground parking with low-clearance restrictions.
Is the Alduo suitable for winter camping?
Yes. The dual-layer mattress and anti-condensation pad provide solid insulation from below. The 2025 model adds a heater port for connecting an external heat source. Pair it with a sleeping bag rated for your expected temperatures and you have a genuinely 4-season setup.
What is the maximum weight the tent can hold?
The tent itself is rated for normal sleeping use by two adults. The limiting factor is your vehicle's roof dynamic load rating — typically 75–100 kg on most vehicles. The tent weighs 71 kg, leaving roughly 30–50 kg for occupants depending on your vehicle. Always check your vehicle manufacturer's specifications.
How does the Alduo compare to soft-shell alternatives?
Key advantages: (1) 30-second setup vs. 3–8 minutes for most soft-shells. (2) 17.5 cm closed height — lower aerodynamic drag and less clearance impact. (3) Aluminum doesn't degrade in UV the way canvas does over years. Trade-off: fixed footprint and at 71 kg it's heavier than most soft-shells (typically 45–60 kg).
What is the return policy?
Standard 14-day return right under EU consumer law. The tent must be unused and in original condition. We strongly recommend contacting us to confirm vehicle compatibility before ordering — we're happy to answer fitment questions at info@snailer.eu or +421 917 449 907.
