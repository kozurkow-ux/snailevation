Common questions

Before you buy.

Will the Alduo fit my vehicle? +
The Alduo fits most vehicles with roof rails or crossbars rated for at least 75 kg dynamic load. The mounting base is 210 × 130 cm with universal sliding brackets — compatible with Thule, Rhino-Rack, Yakima, ARB. No drilling required for most setups. Send us your make and model and we'll confirm within a few hours.
How long does setup really take? +
Under 30 seconds to fully open — the gas struts do the work. Folding takes 1–2 minutes and is a comfortable one-person job. A rubber band keeps the fabric neatly tucked when closed. No poles, no clips, no fumbling in the dark.
Can I leave the Alduo on my car year-round? +
Yes. The aluminum honeycomb shell is corrosion-resistant and UV-stable, designed for permanent mounting. The closed height adds approximately 20 cm to your vehicle height — keep this in mind for underground parking with low-clearance restrictions.
Is it suitable for winter camping? +
Yes. The dual-layer mattress and anti-condensation pad provide solid insulation from below. The 2025 Alduo adds a heater port for connecting an external heat source. Pair with a sleeping bag rated for your temperatures and you have a genuine 4-season setup. Owners have reported comfortable nights at −8°C.
How does the Alduo compare to soft-shell alternatives? +
Key advantages: (1) 30-second setup vs. 3–8 minutes for most soft-shells. (2) 17.5 cm closed height — less drag, less clearance impact. (3) Aluminum doesn't degrade in UV the way canvas does over years. Trade-offs: Fixed footprint, and at 71 kg it's heavier than most soft-shells (45–60 kg). Worth it if speed and durability matter more than weight.
What's the return policy? +
Standard 14-day return right under EU consumer law — unused, original condition. We strongly recommend confirming vehicle compatibility before ordering. Happy to answer fitment questions: info@snailer.eu or +421 917 449 907.
